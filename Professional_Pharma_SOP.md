# STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE (SOP)
## Pharmaceutical Procurement, Manufacturing & Dispatch Workflow

---

# 1. PURPOSE
This SOP defines the end-to-end standardized process for handling Proforma Invoices (PI), generating EOPA numbers, issuing Purchase Orders, ensuring language-specific packing material requirements, tracking material balances, processing dispatch activities, receiving goods at the warehouse, and updating PO fulfillment within the pharmaceutical supply chain.

---

# 2. SCOPE
This SOP applies to:
- Sales / Commercial Team  
- Export / Documentation / EOPA Team  
- Procurement Team  
- Raw Material Vendors  
- Packing Material Vendors  
- Contract Manufacturers  
- Artwork / Regulatory / QA Team  
- Warehouse & Logistics Team  
- Supply Chain / Planning Team  

---

# 3. DEFINITIONS
**Proforma Invoice (PI):** Preliminary sales document listing requested medicines.  
**Proforma Number:** Unique internal reference assigned to PI.  
**EOPA:** Export Order Processing Approval authorizing procurement activities.  
**PO:** Purchase Order for materials or finished goods.  
**RM PO:** Purchase Order for Raw Materials.  
**PM PO:** Purchase Order for Packing Material.  
**FG PO:** Purchase Order for Finished Goods.  
**Language-Based Packing Material:** PM printed according to destination market language.  
**Dispatch Advice (DA):** Document issued prior to shipment.  
**GRN:** Goods Receipt Note created on warehouse receipt.  
**Material Balance Statement:** RM/PM received, consumed, and remaining.  
**Medicine Master:** Vendor, manufacturer, language, and regulatory mapping database for products.  

---

# 4. RESPONSIBILITIES

## Sales / Commercial Team
- Receive PI, assign PI Number, log entry.

## EOPA / Documentation Team
- Review PI, verify export eligibility, issue EOPA number.

## Procurement Team
- Determine PM language.
- Create RM/PM/FG POs.
- Verify RM/PM balance before PO.
- Coordinate vendors and deliveries.

## Contract Manufacturer
- Receive RM/PM.
- Track consumption and balance.
- Issue Finished Goods Dispatch Advice.

## Warehouse Team
- Receive goods.
- Verify and generate GRN.
- Report discrepancies.

## Supply Chain / Planning
- Update PO status.
- Approve PO quantities.
- Validate material balance.

---

# 5. PROCEDURE

## 5.1 Proforma Invoice Receipt
- Partner sends PI.
- Commercial team assigns PI Number (PI/YY-YY/###).
- PI logged in system.

## 5.2 EOPA Number Generation
- EOPA issued (EOPA/YY-YY/###).
- Based only on medicine content.
- Vendor selection determined via Medicine Master.

## 5.3 Procurement Creates Purchase Orders

### 5.3.1 Language Selection for Packing Material
- Determine country-specific language (English/French/Arabic/etc.)
- Select artwork version.
- Trigger artwork approval workflow if needed.

### 5.3.2 Raw Material PO
- Format: PO/RM/YY-YY/####  
- Sent to approved RM vendor.

### 5.3.3 Packing Material PO
- Format: PO/PM/YY-YY/####  
- Includes language + artwork version.

### 5.3.4 Finished Goods PO
- Format: PO/FG/YY-YY/####  
- Specifies FG details and PM version.

## 5.4 Manufacturer Material Receipt
- RM vendors dispatch RM.
- PM vendors dispatch PM.
- Manufacturer records receipts and discrepancies.

## 5.5 Material Consumption & Balance Verification
- Manufacturer maintains RM/PM balance sheet.
- Shares consumption and balance before FG dispatch.
- Procurement calculates:
```
Net Requirement = Total Needed – Available Balance
```
- Supply Chain must approve before issuing any PO.

## 5.6 Dispatch Activities

### RM Dispatch (Vendor → Manufacturer)
Documents:
- RM Dispatch Advice  
- COA  
- Invoice  
- Packing List  

### PM Dispatch (Vendor → Manufacturer)
Documents:
- PM Dispatch Advice  
- Artwork Reference  
- Invoice  
- Packing List  

### FG Dispatch (Manufacturer → Warehouse)
Documents:
- Finished Goods Dispatch Advice  
- Batch details  
- Quantity shipped  

## 5.7 Warehouse GRN
- Verify goods.
- Generate GRN.
- Report shortages/damages.

## 5.8 Warehouse Notification
- GRN  
- Receipt confirmation  
- Discrepancy report  

## 5.9 PO Closure
- Status updated: Fully Fulfilled / Partial / Pending.

---

# 6. NUMBERING LOGIC
- PI Number → Partner-specific.  
- EOPA Number → Medicine-only.  
- PO Numbers → Vendor-specific via Medicine Master.  

Flow:
```
PI → EOPA → Medicine Master → RM/PM/FG POs
```
